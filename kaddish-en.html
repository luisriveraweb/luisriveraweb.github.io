Kaddisch

Oskar Stocker | Luis Rivera, Kollegienkirche, Salzburg 2018

Kaddish, commonly considered a funeral prayer, is actuallly a prayer of thangsgiving, thanking God of receiving mankind. Perhaps I could thank God for receiving a very sick person who has no chance of recovering and is suffering from unberabl pain. But when I think of those who were healthy and innocent, who were murdered or starved or frozen, robbed of life on the basis of their religion, their ethnicity, their political conictions, or their sexual orientation, then I can only feel deep sadness, anger,desperation and incomprehension. At the same time I feel and gladly fulfil, the obligation to live my life with joy and gratitude in this otherwise not so blessed time
